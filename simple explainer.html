The Government's Ban: A Privacy Nightmare

The biggest flaw in the current law is that it forces every single person in Australia—adults, kids, and even tourists—to hand over sensitive ID to tech giants just to use an app.

🚫

Replaces Parents

A blunt, one-size-fits-all government mandate that removes parental choice and judgment.

🛡️

Privacy Risk for All

Creates massive databases of IDs, a prime target for hackers and data breaches.

🔓

Easy to Bypass

Easily defeated with VPNs or by fooling unreliable AI age verification checks.

🚧

A Blunt Prohibition

Punishes all kids by locking them out, rather than teaching them to navigate the digital world safely.

The Alternative: The Parent-Child Digital Safety Link

The PCDSL is a secure, opt-in system that creates a partnership between parents, the government, and online platforms. It's not a ban; it's a sophisticated set of tools that lets parents parent effectively.
