A Smarter Path to Online Safety

The government's social media ban is a flawed solution. There's a better, more practical way that empowers parents instead of replacing them.

The Government's Ban: A Privacy Nightmare

The biggest flaw in the current law is that it forces every single person in Australia—adults, kids, and even tourists—to hand over sensitive ID to tech giants just to use an app.

🚫

Replaces Parents

A blunt, one-size-fits-all government mandate that removes parental choice and judgment.

🛡️

Privacy Risk for All

Creates massive databases of IDs, a prime target for hackers and data breaches.

🔓

Easy to Bypass

Easily defeated with VPNs or by fooling unreliable AI age verification checks.

🚧

A Blunt Prohibition

Punishes all kids by locking them out, rather than teaching them to navigate the digital world safely.

The Alternative: The Parent-Child Digital Safety Link

The PCDSL is a secure, opt-in system that creates a partnership between parents, the government, and online platforms. It's not a ban; it's a sophisticated set of tools that lets parents parent effectively.

A Fundamental Shift in Approach

The Old Way: Govt's Ban

Universal, mandatory control that replaces parental authority and forces everyone to prove their age to private companies.

The PCDSL Way: Parent-Led

Targeted, voluntary supervision that empowers parents who choose to get involved, preserving privacy for everyone else.

How It Works: A Simple 3-Step Process

1

Create Your Secure Account

On your own device, create a single ‘Digital Safety Link’ account via myGov with a one-time identity check.

→
2

Register Your Child's Device

Install the official app on your child's new device, sign in, and allocate it to your child. This locks it into "Child Mode."

→
3

Supervise from Your Hub

That's it. Any new account on that device now appears in your parental hub, ready for you to manage.

The Parent's Toolbox: Real Tools for Real Safety

📊Central Dashboard

See every social media and gaming account your child has on their registered devices, all in one place.

➕Proactive Account Creation

Pre-create safe, supervised accounts for your child on multiple platforms at once with a single click.

🔔Real-Time Alerts

Get notified instantly if your child tries to create a new, unapproved account on their device.

🛠️Intervention Tools

Block users, report content, and reply to comments *as a parent* (your identity is always clear).

📈Real Accountability

Platform responses to your reports are logged with the eSafety Commissioner, creating a performance record.

💬Parent-to-Parent Chat

Securely and anonymously contact other parents through the hub to resolve issues like bullying.

Protecting Children: Built-in Failsafes

🤝

Confidential 'Safe Space' App

The app on the child's device has a discreet function giving them a direct line to Kids Helpline and other support services.

⚖️

A Path to Dispute

A child can confidentially report to the eSafety Commissioner if the parental link is being used abusively.

🎂

Automatic Freedom at 16

On their 16th birthday, all parental supervision and access is automatically and permanently terminated.
